How to Convert Files

Learn how to convert your files quickly and easily with FileForge

Getting Started

Converting files with FileForge is simple and straightforward. Follow these step-by-step instructions to transform your files into any format you need.

1

Upload Your Files

Click on the upload zone or drag and drop your files directly onto the page. You can upload multiple files at once, with a maximum file size of 1GB per file.

2

Choose Output Format

Select the format you want to convert your files to from the dropdown menu. We support images, documents, audio, and video formats.

3

Adjust Quality Settings

Use the quality slider to set the desired output quality. Higher quality means larger file sizes, while lower quality reduces file size.

4

Start Conversion

Click the "Convert Files" button to begin the conversion process. You'll see a progress bar showing the conversion status.

5

Download Results

Once conversion is complete, download your converted files individually or as a ZIP archive containing all files.

Tips for Best Results

Follow these tips to get the best conversion results and optimize your workflow:

Choose the Right Quality

For images, use 80-90% quality for web use and 95-100% for printing. For audio, 320kbps is ideal for music.

Batch Processing

Upload multiple files at once to save time. All files will be converted to the same format and quality settings.

File Security

Your files are automatically deleted from our servers after conversion. For sensitive documents, consider using offline converters.

Optimize File Size

For web use, choose WebP for images and MP4 for videos. These formats provide excellent compression with minimal quality loss.
